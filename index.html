Kids Connect

Play,learn and connect with other kids all for free!

Kids connect is a website where children can learn,play and connect with other children. With the help of fun cartoon characters Kids can learn different subjects as well as socialize and have fun interacting with each other on the playground and in the classroom.

A safe & secure place to play games and learn new skills

Kid connect is a safe and secure place for kids to have fun and grow. Your children can play pre-approved educational games chat with other children online, and use our special storybook features

  • Safe space for kids to read
  • Play games with other kids
  • Chat with other children online
  • Parent can approve or reject requests to chat with their children

Kids connect helps kid stay on top of school,have fun with friends and stay safe

Kids connect is for parents and kids

Everyone can just have fun! Kids Connect features games, videos, homework help, activities, inspirational stories and more.

Go-to place online

Whether you're doing homework, playing games or relaxing with friends, you can do it all on Kids Connect.
